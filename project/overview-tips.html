What is VeVe?

VeVe is a leading digital collectibles marketplace where fans can discover, collect, and interact with officially licensed digital items from their favorite brands and franchises. Using blockchain technology, VeVe ensures each collectible is unique, verifiable, and tradeable within a secure ecosystem.

Main Features

  • Buying: Browse a wide variety of digital collectibles and purchase them instantly using VeVe credits or supported payment methods. Every purchase is recorded on the blockchain for authenticity.
  • Selling: List your collectibles for sale on the marketplace and set your price. The blockchain ensures every transaction is transparent and secure.
  • Trading: Trade collectibles with other users safely within the app, enjoying a dynamic and social collecting experience.
